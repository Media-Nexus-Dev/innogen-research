Anti-aging, Peptide

SNAP-8

$90.00

SNAP-8, also known as Acetyl Octapeptide-3 , has been shown to improve the structure of the skin and decrease the effects of wrinkles caused by the contraction of facial muscles. SNAP-8 is a string of 8 peptides and is commonly compared to the popular wrinkle treatment, Argireline. Energy, Peptide

SS-31

$105.00

SS-31 (Elamipretide) is a synthetic tetrapeptide engineered to selectively target and protect mitochondria, particularly the inner mitochondrial membrane. It binds to cardiolipin, a critical phospholipid, thereby optimizing cytochrome c function within the electron transport chain. This interaction enhances electron flow, increases ATP synthesis, and reduces the generation of reactive oxygen species (ROS). SS-31 accumulates in mitochondria independently of cellular energy status and acts as a ROS scavenger, preventing mitochondrial damage and the release of pro-apoptotic factors such as cytochrome c. Its pharmacokinetic profile allows for both subcutaneous and oral administration, with favorable absorption and safety observed in preclinical studies.

Healing, Immune, Peptide

KPV

$65.00 – $90.00

KPV (Lys-Pro-Val) is a naturally occurring tripeptide derived from the C-terminus of the alpha-melanocyte-stimulating hormone (α-MSH). It retains α-MSH’s potent anti-inflammatory and tissue-protective properties without influencing pigmentation pathways. Preclinical and translational studies show that KPV can suppress inflammatory cytokines (TNF-α, IL-1β, IL-6), block NF-κB activation, and promote epithelial repair in the gut, skin, and mucosal tissues. By modulating melanocortin receptors (MC1R, MC3R) on immune and epithelial cells, KPV exhibits strong cytoprotective, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects, making it a promising compound in inflammation, wound healing, and barrier-integrity research.

Peptide

KLOW

$270.00

KLOW is a multi-pathway regenerative research blend combining GHK-Cu (Glycyl-L-Histidyl-L-Lysine Copper Complex), BPC-157 (Body Protection Compound-157), TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4 fragment), and KPV, a bioactive tripeptide derived from α-melanocyte-stimulating hormone. This formulation integrates extracellular matrix remodeling, angiogenesis, cytoskeletal repair, epithelial barrier stabilization, and targeted anti-inflammatory signaling to support coordinated tissue regeneration. In preclinical and experimental models, KLOW has been associated with accelerated wound healing, enhanced skin and connective tissue repair, improved hair follicle and scalp health, suppression of pro-inflammatory cytokines, and protection against oxidative and inflammation-driven cellular damage. Its combined regenerative and inflammation-modulating profile makes KLOW a compound of interest in dermatological, cosmetic, gastrointestinal, and regenerative biology research.

Our Process

From Synthesis to Your Lab

A transparent, end-to-end process ensures the integrity of every peptide we produce — from raw amino acid to your research bench.

Raw Material Sourcing

We source only pharmaceutical-grade amino acids and reagents from FDA-registered, ISO-certified suppliers with full traceability documentation.

Solid-Phase Synthesis

Peptides are synthesized via automated SPPS with real-time monitoring of coupling efficiency at each residue step.

HPLC Purification

Reverse-phase HPLC removes truncated sequences and impurities, achieving our target purity baseline of 99% or above.

Mass Spec Verification

ESI-MS and MALDI-TOF analysis confirm the exact molecular weight and sequence identity before any batch is released.

Cold-Chain Fulfillment

Lyophilized vials are sealed, labeled with lot number and COA QR code, and shipped with temperature-controlled packaging.
