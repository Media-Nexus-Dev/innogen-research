Our Story

The Standard We Refused to Compromise

Innogen Research was founded on a simple frustration: too many research peptides arrived with inconsistent purity, missing documentation, and no traceability. For serious laboratories, that's unacceptable.

So we built a Canadian, GMP-aligned operation where every vial is synthesized via solid-phase peptide synthesis, purified by HPLC, and verified by mass spectrometry — with a third-party Certificate of Analysis available for every single batch.

Today, leading institutions across Canada rely on Innogen for peptides they can trust at the bench.
