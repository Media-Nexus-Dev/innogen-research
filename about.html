Our Story

The Standard We Refused to Compromise

Innogen Research began with a frustration shared in labs everywhere: research peptides arriving with inconsistent purity, missing documentation, and no traceability. For serious work, that's a non-starter.

So we built a Canadian, GMP-aligned operation around a single promise — every vial is synthesized, purified by HPLC, and verified by mass spectrometry, with a third-party Certificate of Analysis available for every batch.

Today, leading institutions across Canada rely on Innogen for peptides they can trust at the bench.
