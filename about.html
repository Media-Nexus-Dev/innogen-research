Our Story

The Standard We Refused to Compromise

Innogen Research began with a frustration shared in labs everywhere: research peptides arriving with inconsistent purity, missing documentation, and no traceability. For serious work, that's a non-starter.

So we built a Canadian, GMP-aligned operation around a single promise — that every vial is synthesized, purified, and independently verified before it ever reaches your bench.

What started as a small team solving its own problem is now a trusted supplier to laboratories across the country.

Our Journey

How Innogen Grew

From a single bench to a nationwide standard for research-grade peptides.

2023

Founded in Canada

COA on Every Batch
